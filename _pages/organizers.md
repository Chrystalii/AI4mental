<h2>Organizing Committee</h2>

<p>The organizing team brings together complementary expertise across data mining, machine learning, NLP, HCI, and psychology. The organizers have been selected with careful consideration of diversity across multiple dimensions, including geographic representation, gender balance, and career stage.</p>
