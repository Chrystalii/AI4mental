### Proposed Keynote Speech

1. **From Data to Care: How AI and Data Mining Are Transforming Cognitive and Mental Health Support**
2. **Conversational AI and LLMs in Mental Health: Opportunities, Risks, and the Road Ahead**

**Potential Speakers:**
- Ling Feng (Tsinghua)
- Xitong Li (HEC Paris)

### Invited Talk

**Potential Speaker:**
- Nancy Xiaonan Yu (CityUHK) - Health Psychologist

### Panel Discussion

A 45-minute moderated discussion among 4-5 experts on AI-assisted mental health support. Four curated questions will be introduced with brief prompts and contrasting viewpoints, followed by moderated debate, audience Q&A, and live polling among the experts.

### Breakout Discussion

Participants will form small groups to discuss self-selected topics within the workshop theme and identify shared interests and collaboration opportunities. The goal is to surface actionable ideas and seed community-building efforts (e.g., research groups, shared resources).
