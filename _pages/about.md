## About AI4Mental

The **International Workshop on AI for Cognitive and Mental Health Support (AI4Mental)** is a half-day interdisciplinary forum held in conjunction with [KDD 2026](https://kdd2026.kdd.org/).

### Mission

Cognitive and mental health (CMH) disorders are increasingly prevalent worldwide and pose significant societal, clinical, and economic challenges. While conventional mental health support methods remain limited by scalability and reactivity, recent advances in artificial intelligence have opened new opportunities for scalable, proactive, and personalized mental health support.

AI4Mental aims to:

- Consolidate emerging research in AI for cognitive and mental health
- Foster cross-disciplinary dialogue among researchers and practitioners
- Advance trustworthy, effective, and socially responsible AI solutions
- Align with SIGKDD's mission on data science for social good

### Three Pillars

The workshop focuses on three complementary pillars:

1. **AI as Assessment** - Leveraging AI to assess cognitive states, mental health risks, and behavioral patterns
2. **AI as Emotional Support** - AI systems providing empathetic, supportive, and context-aware interactions
3. **AI as Psychological Intervention** - AI-driven interventions supporting treatment, behavior change, and recovery

### Audience

The workshop is designed to attract a diverse and interdisciplinary audience, including:

- Researchers in artificial intelligence, data mining, and natural language processing
- Practitioners in psychology, psychiatry, public health, and digital healthcare
- Industry professionals working on mental health technologies
- Policy makers and regulatory experts

### Format

AI4Mental will be organized as a **half-day on-site workshop** featuring:

- Keynote speeches from leading experts
- Panel discussions with moderated debates
- Long and short paper presentations
- Interactive community-building breakout sessions
